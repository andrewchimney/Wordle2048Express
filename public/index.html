How To Play

Swipe or use arrow keys to create words. Collapse a word by swiping again to get points. Points are determined by the value of each tile destroyed. Words can only be created up to down and left to right.

  • R1

    creates no possible words.

  • K5

    creates a word horizontally.

  • P3

    creates a word vertically.

  • A 1

    creates a word horizontally and vertically.
